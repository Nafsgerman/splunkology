## Persistence Mechanisms

The attackers have established multiple persistent backdoor processes that bind to network sockets and listen for incoming connections from any source (0.0.0.0). The processes use deceptive naming conventions:
- `license_ctrl.e` - Masquerades as a licensing control service
- `ftusbsrvc.exe` - Masquerades as a USB service
- `usbclient.exe` - Masquerades as a USB client utility

These processes demonstrate the attackers' intent to maintain long-term access to the compromised system.
